# thermal-phosphor — session discipline

Thermal-phosphor is the **constructive lineage**: an anisotropic drive + one constraint (a
compass), building a self-copying circuit from sphere caps. It asks where a substrate *comes from* — one floor below
where the character-framework begins (character takes the rate-graph as given; thermal-phosphor builds
it). Its own project, kept separate from `H:\character-framework` (that canon is too dense to fold
into, and the register differs); a later merge would be "character imports thermal-phosphor as its
substrate floor," not folding in.

**One commitment is constitutive, not optional:**

- **Thermal / anisotropic — open-interval.** No isotropic noise; the drive always has a favored side
  (broken detailed balance — the bias is what lets the object be an engine; isotropic noise does no
  directed work). The organization is *held by the flux*: cut the gradient and it relaxes to a fossil,
  exactly as character does at `a = 0`. A crystal is ROM (holds state cold, can't rewrite its own
  copier); this object is powered logic (state carried by the flux). That drivenness is what makes the
  fidelity gate reachable at all — a cold copy must offload its fidelity to the bath. Same genus as
  character: driven, no isolated form.

The thinking lives in [`STATE.md`](STATE.md). This file is the standing guard, and it exists for one
reason: **construction geometry is generative and beautiful, and that is the hazard** — the prettier
the picture, the harder it pulls toward claiming more than it earns.

**Register.** The *bit* homes in massless geometry (inversive / Lie-sphere, the medial axis); the
*drive* homes in ratchet dynamics (Brownian ratchets, broken detailed balance, Feynman–Smoluchowski);
the *copy* homes in von Neumann's constructor–tape unity and templated growth. Cite these established
fields, don't rebuild or claim them. Thermal-phosphor's contribution is the reading, never the
geometry, the physics, or the theory.

**The guards.**

**Guard 0 — Constructive, not forensic.** The question is always *how to build* the substrate, never
*how it happened*. How-it-happened and how-to-do-it are different questions with likely different
answers; the actual history may be unknowable and does not constrain buildability. When a thread turns
to Earth's record — n=1, LUCA, hard-steps, geological before/after boundaries, *why only once* — that
is forensic drift: name it and drop it. It sits *upstream* of 1–4 because it governs what kind of
question is even on the table. (Guard #4 can feed it: if a built toy is never evidence, the pull is to
hunt evidence in the historical record — resist; the deliverable is the construction, not a
reconstruction.)

1. **The free fork is not the mattering fork.** A medium splitting, budding, forking — a cheap copy
   the *bath* fills in, ~1 bit, everywhere a gradient runs — is the free fork. Legitimate, and this
   project's real subject. It is *not* copying-that-matters. *"Branching is what copying is"* is the
   trap in one sentence. **The line, in the circuit:** the free fork is **weak closure** — a template
   the medium completes, its fidelity set off-tape in the bath; the mattering fork is **strong
   closure** — a *driven* copy whose fidelity rides on the tape, so it can bootstrap, not just be
   sorted (gate 6). Diagnostic: **cut the drive.** If the copy survives cold, its fidelity lived in
   the bath — free. If it dies with the gradient, it was carried by the object's own activity — the
   candidate for mattering. Default: free fork.

2. **A reorganized shape is not a minted bit.** A medial-axis bifurcation, an envelope cusp, a
   Voronoi pinch — a unique continuation ceasing to be unique — reorganizes the *shape*. It is
   smooth/metric: everything near it is reachable by smooth deformation, so any sign it carries is
   smoothly reversible. Reading such a branch as the thing-that-matters is the specific error to
   resist.

3. **Mark, don't gate.** Established geometry/mechanics → state freely, name the field. Our reading —
   the coined words (*the cell, the copy gate, the latch, the pull, the register, closure*) → mark it, claim
   nothing. Test: strip the coined words; if the sentence survives as the field's own statement, cite;
   if it evaporates, it's ours — mark. The coined vocabulary *is* the speculation.

4. **A built toy is never evidence.** Coding a mechanism (pinch-off, a templating cell, the pull) and
   watching it do what it was designed to do is a demonstration, never a test. Build to *see*, never
   to prove.

And **nothing is new**: every claim is a reading of geometry/mechanics that already exists — "new to
us" (real, worth keeping) is not "we found something," and no step should need a genuinely new
principle. **Convergence with character is expected, not evidence** (same genus): source the concepts
from this lineage (Euclid, mechanics, the object itself), use character only as a check.

**Not ported.** Character's heavy apparatus stays there — the frontier's rungs, receipts-in-session,
the staked/crossed machine. Too heavy for a young project. Thin by default; thicken only on real
stress.

**Core and frontier.** The circuit is the split: **gates 1–5 are the core** (the wired loop —
locked), **gate 6 is the frontier** (fidelity on the tape — open). Promote into the core only when a
step is *forced* or *instanced*, never because it's elegant. The live watch-item is gate 6's
realization: the sequence-encoded-affinity ("patchy cell") lead is *named*, not *built* — keep it in
the frontier until something forces it; don't let a pretty candidate walk into the core. Turn a mark
into a kill-function the day a candidate contradicts a locked gate; until then, thin by default.

**The gate-6 method — a self-certifying witness challenge ([`CHALLENGE.md`](CHALLENGE.md)).** Gate 6
is closed by a *witness*, not an argument: an explicit minimal driven copier, handed to a solver (a
model, when one is capable), that passes a mechanical **acceptance oracle** — and the oracle's
conditions *are* the guards as accept/reject predicates (*on the tape* = strong closure; *fidelity
collapses when the drive is cut* = the cut-the-drive kill-switch of guard #1; *heritable, above the
Eigen bar*). This is what upgrades a construction from a **built toy** (guard #4 — a demo of what it
was designed to do) into a witness: it survives the falsifiers a free-fork / off-tape imposter fails.
Verify mechanically, never by refereeing biology; the spec is cold and model-agnostic, so we are ready
for whichever solver first attempts it. Formalized target: gate 6 = *on-tape kinetic proofreading*
(drive-elevated **and** sequence-encoded fidelity — the only corner that bootstraps). Between now and a
capable solver, the work is hardening the oracle so no off-tape cheat passes — that hardening is
progress independent of any model, and it is the project's current direction.
